Optional settings for tooltip overlays

Property Documentation

◆ Direction

string? TooltipOptions.Direction
getset

Direction where to open the tooltip

Possible values are:
right, left, top, bottom, center, and auto

auto will dynamically switch between right and left according to the tooltip position on the map
